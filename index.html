Healthcare · Analysis & Prediction

Heart Disease Analysis & Prediction

Analyzed a clinical heart disease dataset across multiple physiological markers, dissecting which features genuinely drive cardiac risk and benchmarking linear vs. tree-based models on structured medical data. Performed data cleaning, feature engineering, encoding, and extensive EDA with correlation heatmaps, distribution plots, and boxplots analyzing both positive correlations (ST depression, vessel blockage, Thalium stress test) and negative correlations (maximum heart rate) with disease risk. Trained and compared 5 models; Ridge Classifier achieved best performance at 92.6%, demonstrating linear models can outperform tree-based approaches on structured clinical data.
